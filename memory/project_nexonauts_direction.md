Original scope (now being cut back): tool directory, marketplace for templates/themes, PeerList-style community, AI tools surface, dashboards, profiles, blog. Result was half-baked across the board.

Revised direction (as of 2026-05-23 discussion):
- Nexonauts main site = marketing + content hub (gobyexample-style developer guides) + product showcase for [[project_recast]] and [[project_docs_compiler]]
- `marketplace/` route reframes to a **resources page**: his own Blogger templates + curated affiliate/referral links (not a two-sided platform)
- `scout/` route reframes to a **bookmarks/inspiration directory**: his own curated UI/tool links (not a community submission system)
- `dev-tools/` may move to `tools.nexonauts.com` subdomain later — undecided
- Community feed, user profiles for strangers, two-sided platform mechanics: dropped
- Subdomain split likely for products (e.g. recast.nexonauts.com) so product UI doesn't contaminate marketing site

**Why:** Solo nights-and-weekends operator cannot bootstrap multiple two-sided network-effect products. Content + product launches compound; platforms need critical mass he can't reach.

**How to apply:** Resist scope creep back toward "platform" features. When suggesting work, prefer content + product polish + tighter scope over new platform mechanics.
